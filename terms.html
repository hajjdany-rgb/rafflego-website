Wallet and Payments

Escrow System

All ticket sale proceeds are held in escrow until the raffle is completed and the prize is delivered. This protects both participants and organizers:

  • Participants are protected because funds are not released until prize delivery is confirmed
  • Organizers are protected because funds are secured before they ship the prize
  • Disputes are resolved before any funds are released

Winner Selection and Fairness

RaffleGo uses a dual-source randomness system to ensure provably fair winner selection:

Source Description
NIST Randomness Beacon U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology public randomness
drand (League of Entropy) Decentralized randomness beacon operated by a global consortium

Every draw result can be independently verified using the public beacon values.

Disclaimer: RaffleGo is not affiliated with or endorsed by NIST or the drand network. We use their publicly available randomness beacons to ensure fair and verifiable winner selection.
