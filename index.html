Fairness & Transparency

How we ensure every draw is provably fair

"RaffleGo does not generate randomness. It verifies publicly published randomness."

Every RaffleGo draw uses dual-source randomness from two independent, publicly auditable sources:

NIST Randomness Beacon

The National Institute of Standards and Technology publishes cryptographically signed random values every 60 seconds. These values are publicly archived and cannot be predicted or manipulated.

drand Distributed Randomness

drand is a decentralized randomness beacon operated by a global network of independent organizations. No single party can predict or influence the output.

Security & Trust

Built with protection at every level

Escrow Protection

All funds are held in escrow until the winner confirms receipt of the prize. Organizers cannot access funds prematurely.

Organizer Security Hold

Organizers must maintain a security deposit to ensure accountability and protect participants from potential issues.

Admin Oversight

Every raffle is reviewed by our admin team before approval. We verify images, descriptions, and prize values to ensure legitimacy.

No Cash Prizes

RaffleGo is for physical prizes only. This reduces fraud risk and ensures tangible value for winners.

Automated Draws

Draws execute automatically using external randomness. No platform employee can influence or predict outcomes.

Dispute Resolution

If issues arise, escrow is frozen until our admin team investigates and resolves the dispute fairly.
