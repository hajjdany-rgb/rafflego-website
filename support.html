Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enter a raffle?

Browse available raffles in the app, select one you're interested in, and purchase tickets using your wallet balance. The more tickets you buy, the better your chances of winning.

How are winners selected?

Winners are selected using a provably fair system that combines randomness from two independent sources: the NIST Randomness Beacon and the drand League of Entropy network. Every draw can be independently verified.

How do I add funds to my wallet?

Go to the Wallet tab in the app and tap "Add Funds." You can add money using a credit/debit card or cryptocurrency.

What happens if I win?

You'll receive an instant notification when you win. The organizer will contact you to arrange prize delivery. Funds are held in escrow until you confirm receipt of your prize.

Can I get a refund on tickets?

Ticket purchases are generally non-refundable once a raffle begins. However, if a raffle is cancelled, you'll receive a full refund to your wallet.

How do I become an organizer?

To create raffles, you need to complete KYC (Know Your Customer) verification. Go to Profile ‚Üí Become an Organizer and follow the verification steps.

Is RaffleGo available in my country?

RaffleGo is available in most countries. However, some regions may have restrictions due to local regulations. Check the app for availability in your area.

Response Time

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please include "URGENT" in your email subject line.
