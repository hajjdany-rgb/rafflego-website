Influencer Neutrality Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Fairness

RaffleGo is committed to providing a level playing field for all users. This policy ensures that influencers, celebrities, and high-profile users receive no preferential treatment in our raffle system.

Core Principle: Every participant has an equal chance of winning, regardless of their social media following, public profile, or relationship with RaffleGo.

2. Equal Treatment Guarantee

RaffleGo guarantees that:

  • No Preferential Odds: Influencers and celebrities have the same odds of winning as any other participant with the same number of tickets.
  • No Reserved Wins: We do not reserve prizes or manipulate draws to favor any user.
  • No Special Access: High-profile users do not receive early access to raffles or exclusive ticket allocations.
  • No Fee Discounts: All users pay the same platform fees regardless of their status.

3. Draw Integrity

Our provably fair draw system ensures neutrality:

  • External Randomness: Winners are selected using publicly verifiable randomness from NIST Beacon and drandâ€”sources that RaffleGo cannot control or influence.
  • Automated Execution: Draws execute automatically without human intervention.
  • Public Verification: Any participant can independently verify draw results using the published randomness values.
  • No Override Capability: RaffleGo employees cannot manually select or change winners.

4. Influencer Organizers

When influencers or celebrities organize raffles on RaffleGo:

  • Same Review Process: Their raffles undergo the same admin approval process as all other organizers.
  • Same Fee Structure: They pay the same tiered platform fees (10%/15%/20%).
  • Same Escrow Rules: Their earnings are held in escrow until winners confirm prize delivery.
  • Same Security Requirements: They must maintain the same security deposits as other organizers.

5. Prohibited Practices

The following practices are strictly prohibited:

  • Fake Giveaways: Organizing raffles with no intention of delivering prizes.
  • Predetermined Winners: Announcing winners before the draw or selecting winners outside the platform.
  • Follower Requirements: Requiring participants to follow social media accounts as a condition of entry (tickets are the only entry method).
  • Exclusive Deals: Offering special terms to influencers that are not available to all users.
  • Promotional Manipulation: Misrepresenting odds or outcomes in promotional content.

6. Disclosure Requirements

Influencers promoting RaffleGo or their raffles must:

  • Clearly disclose any material relationship with RaffleGo
  • Not make false claims about winning odds or guaranteed outcomes
  • Accurately represent how the raffle system works
  • Comply with FTC guidelines and local advertising regulations
  • Not suggest that their followers have better odds of winning

7. Reporting Violations

If you believe this policy has been violated:

  1. Document the suspected violation with screenshots or evidence
  2. Report it through the app's "Report Issue" feature
  3. Or email us at legal@rafflego.co

We investigate all reports and take appropriate action, including account suspension for confirmed violations.

8. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Warning and required corrective action
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Forfeiture of earnings or prizes
  • Public disclosure of violations (in serious cases)

9. Transparency Reports

RaffleGo is committed to transparency. We may publish periodic reports showing:

  • Distribution of winners across user types
  • Verification that draw outcomes match expected statistical distributions
  • Summary of policy enforcement actions taken
